TOP FIVE BEST CITIES IN THE WORLD

LONDON

Multi-ethnic revelry, an enviable variety of small trade and higher education institutions (more than 40 universities and colleges are based in the city) and, finally, the best restaurants for new fans of gastro tours. It is at the top of the rankings. London is the only city in the world to score top marks in all six categories.

NEW YORK

The best city in America and the most European of all American cities can be considered a pioneer of various regulations. The concept of "the city that never sleeps" today contains an unprecedented ambition in creating restrictions. For a place for future generations.

PARIS

Loyalty to Paris is close to the heart of the sybarite and the globetrotter. They want to come back here. Despite the terrorist attacks, the Parisians do not give up their cheerful life and unsurpassed pride in the thousand-year-old city: cultural, creative and comfortable.

ODESSA

Odessa is a port city on the Black Sea in southern Ukraine. It is known for its beaches and 19th century architecture, such as the building of the Odessa Opera and Ballet Theatre. The Potemkin Stairs, which gained worldwide fame thanks to the movie "Battleship Potemkin", leads to the sea, where the Vorontsovsky lighthouse is located. Primorsky Boulevard stretches along the coast. Here you can walk and admire the coastal mansions and monuments.

SINGAPORE

Over the past 50 years, the city-state of Singapore has grown at a rapid pace, from a politically unstable, resource-intensive and unskilled ex-colony to the world's busiest transportation hub, financial capital and global economic outpost in Asia. Brand engineering at the most ambitious level.
